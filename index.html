Analysis of Differentiable Piano model

The current webpage offers multiply audio examples from the TI-Project "Analysis of Differntiable Piano Model". The aim was to figure out what are physically informed neural based synthesizer are caplable of. Thus, we tried to analyze the underlaying piano synthesizer[1] which is also available on Github ddsp-piano .


[1] L. Renault, R. Mignot, and A. Roebel, “Differentiable Piano Model for MIDI-to-Audio Performance Synthesis,” Sep. 2022, publisher: Differentiable Piano Model
